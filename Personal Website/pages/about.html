Hi! My name is Zach Marino and I am a second year computer science and design student at Northeastern University. I'm from Highland Park, Illinois but I was born in Philadelphia (go phils!).

As both a computer science and design student, I get the opportunity to not only design but code the projects I am passionate about. I am concentrating in interaction design which has given me a strong skillset using tools like Balsamiq and Figma, as well as a general affinity for the UI/UX design process. In the software world, this translates to a unique perspective on the software design process which you would not see from a typical developer.

After class I am involved in multiple clubs on campus. First and foremost I am a product lead for Northeastern Electric Racing, our school's F1 team. We have an in-house project management web app called FinishLine made in Typescript and React which I contribute design work to and manage other designers. I am also on the E-board for the Musicheads club, where we talk about and listen to music every week. We are currently in the process of becoming an official club.

In my free time, I like listening to music, watching new movies, working on art projects, and watching baseball!
